About Us

Card Crew is dedicated to learning and playing all sorts of
card games. We hold weekly meetings where we teach and play new
games such as Bridge, Rummy, Poker, Big 2, as well as some of our own creations!

Weekly Meetups

Weekly meetups are held every Thursday from 1pm-4pm. Currently we are holding our sessions through Discord, and our online arsenal of games includes but is not limited to: Among Us, Poker, Big 2, Sushi Go and more! Come along to see what we're about..
